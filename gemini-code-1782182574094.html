What we do

Our Services

01🖨️

Custom 3D Printing

We print your vision with precision. From functional parts to display pieces, every order gets the same meticulous attention. We work across a range of materials and finishes — PLA, PETG, resin, and more — to match your requirements exactly.

02✏️

3D Design & Modelling

Bring a sketch, a photo, or just an idea. Our design team builds a precise, print-ready 3D model from scratch and walks you through approval before anything goes to print. No CAD experience needed on your end.

03⚙️

Prototyping

Validate your concept before committing to production. Our rapid prototyping gets you a physical model fast — test, refine, and be confident in your design. Perfect for engineers, inventors, and product developers.

04🔧

Repairs & Replacement Parts

Broken components. Discontinued parts. Vintage pieces with no supplier. If it can be measured and modelled, we can reproduce it — often better than the original. Bring the broken piece and we'll do the rest.

Not sure which service fits your needs? Message us directly — we'll point you in the right direction at no cost.

Who we are

About Khalsa3D

Khalsa3D is a New Zealand-based custom 3D printing studio built on one principle: every order matters. Whether you need a single prototype or a batch of custom parts, you get the same dedication and quality that's earned us a perfect 5-star reputation.

We work with individuals, businesses, makers, and engineers — bringing ideas to life with precision and speed. From complex geometries to simple replacement parts, nothing is too small or too intricate for us to take on.

Every print is quality-checked before it leaves our hands. That's not just a promise — it's why our reviews speak for themselves. When you order from Khalsa3D, you're getting craftsmanship backed by genuine care.
